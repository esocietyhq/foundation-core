# Output Blueprint — Foundation

Foundation produces a structured blueprint from raw input.

---

## Blueprint Sections

### 1. System Overview
A clear explanation of what the idea is.

### 2. Core Purpose
The central reason the idea exists.

### 3. Audience
The person, group, or market the system serves.

### 4. Problem Statement
The specific problem being addressed.

### 5. Value Proposition
The useful result created by the system.

### 6. Functional Components
The major parts needed for the system to work.

### 7. Execution Path
The first practical steps required to move forward.

### 8. Risks and Constraints
The weak points, limits, assumptions, or missing pieces.

### 9. Final Direction
The clearest next move.

---

## Output Goal

The output should make the idea easier to understand, build, explain, and execute.
